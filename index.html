Moment to imagine:

Your specialist works with a pool of 3,000 different keywords while optimizing your campaign. He made 300 changes and put them down for example on Google Sheets. Next, he looks at the resulting changes in the PPC campaign and sees that there is a result. But ... out of 300 changes ... which one exactly led to the result? Most likely, an experienced PPC specialist has an idea of ​​what works and what does not, but the one will not be able to give an accurate answer. This would require making each change separately and observing the result. Thus, a good specialist can really test quality but would be forced to confine 2-6 tests per day. In addition, there is always a chance a specialist will make a mistake, because it is impossible to independently and ideally control such a huge data flow. However, our unique software solution is never wrong. It clearly detects what changes have been made, and what changes have affected the result. As a result, with such a solution, we can carry out 100 tests a day, and still see the result of each of them.
